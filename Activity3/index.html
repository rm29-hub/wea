Rules of Badminton:

  1. A match consists of the best of 3 games of 21 points.
  2. At 20 all, the side which gains a 2 point lead first, wins that game.
  3. To score a point, the shuttlecock must hit within the parameters of the opponent's court
  4. In serving, make sure to strike the shuttle below the waist and from the correct service court, ensuring the racket head points downward.
